About Leo Wemyss

Discover the journey and expertise of Leo Wemyss, your dedicated fitness instructor.

My Journey

With over 20 years of experience in the fitness industry, I have dedicated my life to helping others achieve their fitness goals. My journey started as a personal trainer, and over the years, I have developed a deep understanding of the human body, exercise techniques, and nutrition.

Throughout my career, I have worked with a diverse range of clients, from beginners to professional athletes. My personalized approach ensures that each client receives the attention and guidance they need to succeed. Whether it's through one-on-one training sessions, group classes, or online coaching, my goal is to empower you to become the best version of yourself.
