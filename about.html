About Leo Wemyss

Professional fitness instructor with over 20 years of experience.

Highly Experienced Fitness Professional

Leo Wemyss is a dedicated fitness instructor with an impressive background spanning over 20 years. His expertise covers a wide range of disciplines, making him a well-rounded trainer who can cater to various fitness goals.

Throughout his career, Leo has honed his skills in strength training, cardiovascular conditioning, and flexibility exercises. He has a deep understanding of exercise science and anatomy, enabling him to design safe and effective workouts for clients of all levels. Leo's passion for fitness is infectious, and he motivates his clients to push their limits and achieve their best results.

Kickboxing and Self-Defense Instructor

Leo brings extensive experience in kickboxing and self-defense, empowering individuals with practical skills and the confidence to protect themselves. He provides a welcoming and supportive environment where students can learn valuable techniques while improving their fitness.

Military Background

Leo's prior service in the Irish Army has instilled in him a strong work ethic, discipline, resilience, and a deep understanding of physical fitness. He applies these values to his training methods, fostering a sense of accomplishment and pushing clients to achieve their full potential.

Through his personalized training sessions, Leo tailors his instruction to meet the unique needs and goals of each student. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics of self-defense or an experienced martial artist seeking to refine your skills, Leo offers a comprehensive approach that combines physical training with mental conditioning. His passion for empowering others shines through in every class, as he inspires students to not only become stronger and more confident but also to embrace a healthier and more active lifestyle.

One on One Personal Instructor

Leo's mastery extends beyond kickboxing, encompassing world-class instruction in Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and Krav Maga. This fusion of striking, grappling, and practical self-defense techniques creates a uniquely dynamic and effective training program. Through personalized sessions, you'll develop well-rounded combat skills, enhance your physical fitness, improve coordination, and cultivate the confidence to protect yourself in any situation.

Journey and Achievements

1993: Started Fitness Journey

Leo began his journey into the world of fitness and quickly developed a passion for helping others achieve their health goals.

2001: Joined the Irish Army

Leo's military service instilled in him the values of discipline and resilience, which he brings into his training sessions.

2010: Certified Personal Trainer

Leo became a certified personal trainer, specializing in strength and conditioning, martial arts, and self-defense.

2015: Founded Wemyss Workouts

Leo founded Wemyss Workouts to provide personalized and group fitness training to individuals of all fitness levels.

2020: Expanded Services

Leo expanded his services to include online training programs and virtual classes to reach a wider audience.

2021: Youth Coaching

Leo expanded his services to include youth coaching programs, nurturing the next generation of athletes.
