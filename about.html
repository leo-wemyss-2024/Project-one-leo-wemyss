About Leo Wemyss

Highly Experienced Fitness Professional

Leo Wemyss is a dedicated fitness instructor with an impressive background spanning over 20 years. His expertise covers a wide range of disciplines, making him a well-rounded trainer who can cater to various fitness goals.

Kickboxing and Self-Defense Instructor (20+ Years)

Leo brings extensive experience in kickboxing and self-defense, empowering individuals with practical skills and the confidence to protect themselves.
He provides a welcoming and supportive environment where students can learn valuable techniques while improving their fitness.

Military Background (24 Years)

Leo's prior service in the Irish Army has instilled in him a strong work ethic, discipline, resilience, and a deep understanding of physical fitness.
He applies these values to his training methods, fostering a sense of accomplishment and pushing clients to achieve their full potential.

World-Class Martial Arts Instructor

Leo's expertise extends to world-class martial arts instruction in Muay Thai, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and Krav Maga.
This unique blend of combat techniques and physical conditioning provides a dynamic and effective training program that improves overall fitness, coordination, and self-defense skills.

Personal Training

Leo offers personalized training plans tailored to individual needs and goals. Whether you're looking to lose weight, gain muscle, improve strength and conditioning, or train for a specific event, Leo will create a customized program that gets you results.
He takes the time to understand your fitness level, limitations, and goals, ensuring a safe and effective training experience.

Strength & Conditioning Coach

Leo excels in strength and conditioning coaching. He utilizes various training methods to improve your strength, power, speed, agility, and cardiovascular endurance.
